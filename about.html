About Eve

  • Eve started his career on Niconico in October 2009, initially using the names Kurowa and Keitora. After being part of the band Einie and later "RIOT OF COLOR," he released his debut album, Wonder Word, in 2015.
  • Subsequent albums like Official Number and Bunka followed, with the latter peaking at 14 on the Oricon Albums Chart. In 2018, Eve collaborated with Sou on the album Blue. In 2019, his album Otogi achieved significant chart success, reaching 6 on the Oricon Albums Chart.
  • Eve's major breakthrough came with Smile in 2020, peaking at 2 on the Oricon Albums Chart. Following this, he released the EP Kaikai Kitan/Ao no Waltz in December 2020, featuring tracks used in popular anime.
  • In 2022, Netflix announced the musical film "Adam by Eve: A Live in Animation," premiered in March. Eve's third major album, Kaizin, followed, peaking at no. 4 on the Oricon Albums Chart. Additionally, he contributed to the anime film Bubble and released the single "Fight Song" for Chainsaw Man in December 2022.
